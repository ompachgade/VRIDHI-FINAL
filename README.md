# Project Introduction: Vridhi (वृद्धि)

The Vision: In India, we often know how much we earn, but struggle with exactly where to put it to build long-term wealth. Vridhi is not an expense tracker—it is a proactive Financial Allocation and Growth Engine designed to map out exactly what you should do with your money from the moment you earn it.

What is Vridhi? Vridhi (meaning Growth or Increase) is a smart financial planning web platform. You simply input your monthly income, and Vridhi does the heavy lifting, instantly calculating how to distribute your money into specific, actionable buckets for maximum security and growth.

Our Core Mission: To move users from "guessing" to "growing." We eliminate the stress of budgeting by applying structured, proven financial rules, showing users exactly how much to save, hold for emergencies, spend on necessities, and invest.

Key Features for our Team to Build:

The Vridhi Allocator (The 20-20-30-30 Rule): A clean, intuitive UI where users enter their income, and the platform automatically divides it into four distinct financial pillars. For every ₹100 earned, Vridhi structures it as:

20% Secure Savings: Capital set aside for short-term goals and planned future purchases.

20% Emergency Shield: A highly liquid safety net for medical or unexpected life events.

30% Home & Essentials: Coverage for daily living expenses, household needs, EMIs, and personal loans.

30% Wealth Generation: Dedicated capital exclusively for investments.

Smart Investment Recommender: Once the 30% investment bucket is calculated, Vridhi doesn't leave the user hanging. It provides tailored, actionable investment ideas based on that specific amount, suggesting options like Small-ticket FDs, Index Mutual Funds, SIPs, or Digital Gold.

Financial Health Dashboard: A clear, visual breakdown using modern charts and graphs that displays the user's ideal financial distribution, making complex wealth-building concepts easy to digest for anyone.

The Goal: To become the most trusted digital financial companion in India, turning your monthly income into a clear, actionable roadmap for long-term Vridhi (Growth).
